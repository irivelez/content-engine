# LinkedIn Post Templates

> **Note**: This is a starter template library. You'll add your own templates as you discover what works for your niche and voice.

## Overview
This library contains fill-in-the-blank templates for different LinkedIn post types. Use these as starting points, then customize with your voice and values.

---

## Personal Story Templates

### Template 1: The Transformation Story
```
[Timeframe] ago, I was [struggling state]

[One specific example of the struggle]

Today, I'm [achieved state]

Here's what changed:

1. [Key change 1]
   [One-line elaboration]

2. [Key change 2]
   [One-line elaboration]

3. [Key change 3]
   [One-line elaboration]

The truth is, [counterintuitive insight about the journey]

[CTA question about their journey]
```

**Use when**: Sharing personal growth, building credibility, inspiring action

---

### Template 2: The Failure Lesson

## Template Selection Guide

### By Goal:
- **Build authority**: Framework Share, Case Study, Industry Commentary
- **Drive engagement**: Open Question, Poll-Style, Unpopular Opinion
- **Provide value**: Listicle, Curated List, Resource Offer
- **Build connection**: Personal Story, Failure Lesson, Permission Grant
- **Start conversations**: Myth-Buster, Pattern Recognition

### By Audience Stage:
- **New followers**: Educational, Resource-Sharing
- **Engaged community**: Contrarian, Personal Story
- **Mixed audience**: Listicle, Framework Share

### By Time Investment:
- **Quick (10 min)**: One-Liner Thread, Quote Commentary
- **Medium (20-30 min)**: Most templates
- **Deep (45+ min)**: Case Study, Long-form Story

## Customization Guidelines

### How to Make Templates Your Own:

1. **Add your voice**: Replace placeholder language with how you'd actually say it
2. **Adjust structure**: Move sections, combine elements, break patterns
3. **Insert personality**: Add your unique phrases, metaphors, examples
4. **Match your niche**: Adapt examples and references to your industry
5. **Test and iterate**: Track which templates get best engagement

### Template Mixing:
Combine elements from multiple templates:
- Personal Story hook + Framework body + Resource Offer CTA
- Myth-Buster opening + Case Study proof + Open Question CTA

## Spanish Adaptation Notes

When adapting these templates to Spanish:

**Consider**:
- Tone shifts (Spanish LinkedIn often more personal/warm)
- Cultural references (use relevant examples)
- Formality (tú vs usted - usually tú for LinkedIn)
- Sentence structure (Spanish allows longer sentences naturally)
- Idioms (don't translate literally, find Spanish equivalents)

**Example**:
```
English: "I learned this the hard way"
Spanish: "Lo aprendí de la manera difícil" ❌ (literal)
Spanish: "Lo aprendí a las malas" ✓ (natural idiom)
```

## Instructions for AI Agents

When using templates:

1. **Choose based on goal**: What's the post trying to achieve?
2. **Customize heavily**: Templates are starting points, not scripts
3. **Preserve voice**: Author's unique style > template purity
4. **Mix when appropriate**: Combine template elements if it serves the content
5. **Explain your choice**: Tell the author which template you used and why

**Remember**: The best posts use template structure with authentic content. Never sacrifice authenticity for template adherence.
