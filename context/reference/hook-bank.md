# Hook Bank

> Example hooks organized by use case. Agents: follow the EXAMPLES, not just the patterns.
> Source: Literally Academy (Lara Acosta) — Winning Hooks collection.

---

## How to Use

1. Find the category that matches your post angle
2. Pick an example hook that fits
3. Adapt with your specifics (tool name, number, timeframe)
4. Write the rest of the post in Spanish

---

## Category 1: "I Did X" (Results & Proof)

Use when you built something, tested something, or got a specific result.

**Example hooks from top creators:**

- Here's how I achieved [notable accomplishment] in [timeframe].
- I've tested this with 50+ businesses. It works every time.
- This method got me a 92% client retention rate.
- I built a 7-figure brand without ads — here's how.
- This advice made me my first $10K online.
- What I'm about to teach made my client $60K in 2 weeks.
- I get paid thousands just to do this one thing — and you can too.
- This is the exact script I use to close high-ticket deals.

**Patterns you can reuse:**

```
Here's how I achieved {accomplishment} in {timeframe}.
I've tested this with {number}+ {people/businesses}. It works every time.
This {method/tool} got me {specific metric}.
I built {thing} without {expected requirement} — here's how.
```

## Category 2: "Stop / Don't Do This" (Mistakes & Corrections)

Use when calling out a bad practice and offering the fix.

**Example hooks from top creators:**

- I've audited hundreds of accounts — this is the #1 mistake.
- Most people overcomplicate this. Here's the simple version that works.
- I've managed million-dollar ad budgets — here's what never works.
- The biggest mistake 90% of [ICP] make:
- The 1 mistake I made when I started [doing thing].
- Stop [doing hard thing] for [business].
- I've reviewed thousands of sales calls — this intro always works best.

**Educational hook patterns:**

```
8 ways to increase/decrease {outcome} in {industry}:
Stop {doing hard thing} for {business}.
{Number} {solutions} I use in every {work related thing}:
The biggest mistake 90% of {ICP} make:
The 1 mistake I made when I started {doing thing}.
```

## Category 3: "After X, I Noticed" (Authority Through Experience)

Use when sharing patterns or insights from accumulated experience.

**Example hooks from top creators:**

- After working with 100+ clients, I noticed this pattern…
- After 7 years in this industry, here's what no one tells you.
- I've coached 1,000+ people and here's what most get wrong.
- I've spent years studying this — here's what I found.
- I've helped creators scale from 0 to 100K — here's what works.
- I've spoken on stages across 5 countries — here's what I always say first.

**Patterns you can reuse:**

```
After {experience/number}, I noticed this pattern…
After {time} in {field}, here's what no one tells you.
I've {done thing} {number}+ times and here's what most get wrong.
I've spent {time} studying this — here's what I found.
```

## Category 4: "How To / Step by Step" (Teaching)

Use when teaching a process you've actually done.

**Example hooks from top creators:**

- How I create [hard thing] for [industry].
- How to do a [hard thing] in 5 steps.
- How to actually [do hard/desired thing] from your [business].
- This took me a decade to figure out — you get it in 60 seconds.
- If you want results like these, here's where to start.
- This framework made me more money than any degree ever could.

**Patterns you can reuse:**

```
How I {create/build/do} {hard thing} for {context}.
How to {do hard thing} in {number} steps.
How to actually {desired thing} from your {starting point}.
This took me {time} to figure out — you get it in {short time}.
```

## Category 5: "Contrarian / I Changed My Mind" (Hot Takes)

Use when challenging common advice or sharing an evolved opinion.

**Example hooks from top creators:**

- I used to teach this — but I was wrong. Here's what I believe now.
- Here's what top performers do *differently* from everyone else.
- As a [profession], here's my take on [trending topic].
- This is what I teach my private clients who pay me $10K/month.

**Patterns you can reuse:**

```
I used to {believe/teach} this — but I was wrong. Here's what I {believe/do} now.
Here's what {top performers/experts} do *differently* from everyone else.
As a {profession}, here's my take on {trending topic}.
```

## Category 6: "System / My Process" (Hacks & Workflows)

Use when showing how your system works or sharing a shortcut.

**Example hooks from top creators:**

- This is the strategy top performers use daily.
- I've built systems that run without me — this is how you buy back time.
- This one principle changed how I run every business.
- My 1 secret weapon I used as an [x].
- I've made [specific number] in [timeframe]. The secret? [reveal].
- The secret to a powerful [x].

**Educational hook patterns:**

```
{Number} {solutions} I use in every {work related thing}:
This is the strategy {who} use daily.
I've built systems that {result} — this is how.
My 1 secret weapon I used as a {role}.
```

## Category 7: "Transformation" (Before → After)

Use when showing personal or professional evolution.

**Example hooks from top creators:**

- The biggest shift that took me from average to expert…
- This one mindset shift turned me from a freelancer to a CEO.
- This is what I teach my private clients who pay me $10K/month.

**Patterns you can reuse:**

```
The biggest shift that took me from {before} to {after}…
{Time} ago, I {was struggling state}. Today, I {achieved state}.
This one {change/shift} turned me from {before} to {after}.
```

## Quick Reference

| Your post is about... | Use Category |
|---|---|
| Something you built or tested | 1: "I Did X" |
| A bad practice to correct | 2: "Stop / Don't Do This" |
| Patterns from experience | 3: "After X, I Noticed" |
| Teaching a process | 4: "How To" |
| Challenging common advice | 5: "Contrarian" |
| Your workflow or tools | 6: "System / My Process" |
| Your personal journey | 7: "Transformation" |

## Anti-Patterns (Philosopher Mode — AVOID)

- "Reflexión de media noche..."
- "La simplicidad es un skill subestimado..."
- "El éxito no es lo que crees..."
- "3 verdades que nadie quiere escuchar..."
- "El secreto del éxito es..."

**Test**: Can you follow the hook with "because I did X and Y happened"? If not, rewrite or kill it.

## Instructions for Agents

1. **Use the EXAMPLES as models** — don't invent hook styles that aren't in this file
2. **Specificity wins** — always replace placeholders with real numbers, tools, timeframes
3. **Builder Mode is non-negotiable** — every hook must lead to something built/done/learned
4. **Adapt to Spanish naturally** — the examples are in English for reference, final posts are always in Spanish
5. **Check voice-and-style.md** for Irina's formatting preferences
