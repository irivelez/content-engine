## What Works

### 1. Resource Curation (TOP Performer)

**Formula**: "Las [X] de [topic] publicaron [resource]. Gratis. Y casi nadie los está aprovechando."

**Why it works**:
- High value density + FOMO
- Personal filter ("yo uso X para Y")
- Share-for-others CTA ("Repostéalo para que...")
- Creates viral mechanic

**Visual**: Canva design showing resources at a glance

**Example**: AI Playbooks post
- Hook: "Las 4 empresas de IA más grandes publicaron sus playbooks. Gratis. Y casi nadie los está aprovechando."
- Outstanding performance (My Top)
- URL: https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7397093021651025920/

---

### 2. Resistance + Breakthrough

**Formula**: "Me había resistido a [tool] hasta que no pude más."

**Why it works**:
- Relatability (audience has same resistance)
- Vulnerability + authority balance
- Actionable method shared
- Specific result achieved

**Visual**: Screenshot or demo of result

**Example**: n8n post
- Hook: "Me había resistido a n8n hasta que no pude más."
- Method: Using Claude as specialized n8n assistant
- High engagement (My Best)
- URL: https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7350670598143995904/

---

### 3. Personal Milestone + Reverse Timeline

**Formula**: "Hace X tiempo [milestone]" + connecting dots backward

**Why it works**:
- Social proof without bragging
- Vulnerability ("perdi el miedo de ser principiante")
- Shows deliberate path (not luck)
- Reflective CTA for reader

**Visual**: Photos from the milestone

**Example**: Andrew Ng post
- Hook: "Hace 3 semanas conocí a Andrew Ng"
- Reverse timeline of decisions that led there
- 144 likes, 22 comments (My Best)
- URL: https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7315829607893454849/

---

### 4. Demo/Builder Posts

**Formula**: "Acabo de construir [X] en [tiempo]"

**Why it works**:
- Builder mode (doing, not thinking)
- Specific timeframe creates credibility
- Concrete result to point to
- Inspires audience to try

**Visual**: Demo video or screenshot

**Example**: Gemini app post
- Hook: "Acabo de construir una app con Gemini 3 en 5 minutos."
- High engagement (+11K impressions)
- URL: https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7347821821651025920/

## What Doesn't Work

### 🎯 Builder vs Philosopher (THE CRITICAL PATTERN)

**This is the #1 performance predictor.**

**BUILDER mode** (✅ 250+ engagement, 11K+ impressions):
- "Acabo de construir una app con Gemini en 5 minutos"
- "Me había resistido a n8n hasta que..."
- "Los contenedores comparten el kernel, las VMs no"
- Grounded in: **doing, building, learning by action**

**PHILOSOPHER mode** (❌ 58-100 engagement):
- "Reflexión de media noche: la tecnología necesita problemas"
- "Simplicidad es un skill a desarrollar"
- "Steve Jobs dijo que la iteración es importante"
- Grounded in: **thinking about concepts, borrowed wisdom, abstract ideas**

**Rule**: Your audience follows the BUILDER, not the philosopher.

**Before posting, ask**: "What did I BUILD/FIND/DO?" If you can't answer with something specific, reframe or kill the idea.

---

### Never Do This:

1. **English posts**
   - Evidence: Vibe Coding post = WORST performer ever (catastrophic)
   - Why it fails: Audience is Spanish-speaking, violates audience contract
   - Status: NEVER post in English on LinkedIn

2. **"Reflexión de media noche" framing**
   - Evidence: Consistent low performance (58/100)
   - Why it fails: Signals random thought, not structured insight
   - Status: KILL this framing immediately

3. **Encyclopedia lists without grounding**
   - Evidence: "7 AI Concepts" - 12 likes, 0 comments
   - Why it fails: No "I used this when..." connection, presents as expert not learner
   - Status: Every concept needs personal anchor

4. **Borrowed authority**
   - Evidence: Steve Jobs quote posts underperform
   - Why it fails: Audience wants YOUR experience, not borrowed wisdom
   - Status: Reference others only if tied to your specific experience

5. **Generic motivation**
   - Evidence: "Alejarte del ruido" with sunset photo (72/100)
   - Why it fails: Could be posted by anyone, no tech value, zero Irina voice
   - Status: NO generic life advice without tech substance

6. **Philosopher mode**
   - Evidence: Abstract concepts without doing/building anchor fail consistently
   - Why it fails: Audience wants to see you BUILD, not think about building
   - Status: Always ground in specific action taken
