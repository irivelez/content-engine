## What Works

### 1. Resource Curation (TOP Performer)

**Formula**: "Las [X] de [topic] publicaron [resource]. Gratis. Y casi nadie los está aprovechando."

**Why it works**:
- High value density + FOMO
- Personal filter ("yo uso X para Y")
- Share-for-others CTA ("Repostealo para que...")
- Creates viral mechanic

**Visual**: Canva design showing resources at a glance

**Example**: AI Playbooks post
- Hook: "Las 4 empresas de IA mas grandes publicaron sus playbooks. Gratis. Y casi nadie los esta aprovechando."
- Outstanding performance (My Top)
- URL: https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7397093021651025920/

---

### 2. Resistance + Breakthrough

**Formula**: "Nunca me gusto [tool]. Ahora puedo [result] sin [pain point]." OR "Me habia resistido a [tool] hasta que no pude mas."

**Why it works**:
- Relatability (audience has same resistance)
- Vulnerability + authority balance
- Actionable method shared
- Specific result achieved

**Visual**: Screenshot or demo video of result

**Examples**:
- **n8n Resistance** (original): "Me habia resistido a n8n hasta que no pude mas." (My Best)
  URL: https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7350670598143995904/
- **n8n Prototipar** (evolution): "Nunca me gusto n8n. Ahora puedo prototipar flujos sin tocarlo." (My Good)
  URL: https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7423477654542163968/
  - Shows the pattern REPLICATES: same tool, evolved angle, still performs

**Pattern note**: The n8n resistance pattern has now been used twice successfully. The second version (My Good) evolved from pure resistance to resistance + concrete outcome. Both work.

---

### 3. Personal Milestone + Reverse Timeline

**Formula**: "Hace X tiempo [milestone]" + connecting dots backward

**Why it works**:
- Social proof without bragging
- Vulnerability ("perdi el miedo de ser principiante")
- Shows deliberate path (not luck)
- Reflective CTA for reader

**Visual**: Photos from the milestone

**Example**: Andrew Ng post
- Hook: "Hace 3 semanas conoci a Andrew Ng"
- Reverse timeline of decisions that led there
- 144 likes, 22 comments (My Best)
- URL: https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7315829607893454849/

### 4. Demo/Builder Posts

**Formula**: "Acabo de construir [X] en [tiempo]" OR "[X] pasos. 0 lineas de codigo. 1 app"

**Why it works**:
- Builder mode (doing, not thinking)
- Specific timeframe creates credibility
- Concrete result to point to
- Inspires audience to try

**Visual**: Demo video or screenshot

**Examples**:
- **Gemini app** (original): "Acabo de construir una app con Gemini 3 en 5 minutos." (My Good - reclassified from Best)
  URL: https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7396743153359241216/
- **5 pasos app**: "5 pasos. 0 lineas de codigo. 1 app" (My Best) -- NEW
  URL: https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7423360666444513280/
  - Variant of the formula: numbers-first hook instead of "Acabo de construir"
  - Video format with demo

**Pattern note**: The "5 pasos" variant outperformed the original "Acabo de construir" formula. Numbers-first hooks ("5 pasos. 0 lineas. 1 app.") may be a stronger variant because they create more curiosity and feel more structured.

---

### 5. Discovery/Hidden Feature Posts (NEW - CONFIRMED)

**Formula**: "Llevo [tiempo] usando [tool]. Pero hasta hace [tiempo] descubri [feature]"

**Why it works**:
- Creates curiosity gap: "if SHE didn't know, maybe I don't either"
- Positions Irina as learner AND discoverer
- Specific tool + specific feature = high practical value
- Builder mode: grounded in actual daily usage

**Visual**: Screenshot or image of the feature

**Example**: Claude Code ctrl+z post
- Hook: "Llevo meses usando Claude Code. Pero hasta hace 3 semanas descubri el ctrl+z de Claude"
- Outstanding performance (My Top)
- URL: https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:7422868313560514560/

**Replicable formula**: "Llevo [tiempo] usando [tool]. Pero hasta hace [tiempo] descubri [hidden feature/trick]"

## What Doesn't Work

### Builder vs Philosopher (THE CRITICAL PATTERN)

**This is the #1 performance predictor.**

**BUILDER mode** (My Top / My Best / My Good):
- "Llevo meses usando Claude Code. Pero hasta hace 3 semanas descubri el ctrl+z" (My Top)
- "Las 4 empresas de IA mas grandes publicaron sus playbooks" (My Top)
- "5 pasos. 0 lineas de codigo. 1 app" (My Best)
- "Acabo de construir una app con Gemini en 5 minutos" (My Good)
- "Nunca me gusto n8n. Ahora puedo prototipar flujos sin tocarlo." (My Good)
- "2 semanas de hype. 30 minutos de reverse engineering de ClawdBot" (My Good)
- Grounded in: **doing, building, discovering, learning by action**

**PHILOSOPHER mode** (My Worst -- 100% correlation):
- "Reflexion de media noche: la tecnologia necesita problemas" (My Worst)
- "Un nino que nazca hoy nunca sera mas inteligente que la IA" (My Worst)
- "Idealizamos otras latitudes, pero los problemas son mas parecidos" (My Worst)
- "Nada mas que decir: A veces solo necesitas alejarte del ruido" (My Worst)
- "Stop chasing complexity. Tu cerebro ama complicar lo simple." (My Worst) -- NEW
- "Siempre que escucho a Geoffrey Hinton, es inevitable no quedarme reflexiva." (My Worst) -- NEW
- Grounded in: **thinking about concepts, borrowed wisdom, abstract ideas**

**MIXED mode** (Philosopher with Builder elements -- still underperforms):
- "Anthropic se puso la 10 con respecto a los Ads" (My Worst) -- Commentary on company decision, not grounded in building
- "Solo en San Francisco / OpenClaw event" (My Worst) -- Event recap without specific technical takeaway
- "Muchas apps con IA solo estan usando la mitad del poder de los prompts" (My Worst) -- Technical claim without personal grounding
- "La autonomia: the ultimate goal de la IA" (My Worst) -- Educational explainer without personal anchor -- NEW
- "Tu idea podria construirse en vivo este viernes" (My Worst) -- Promotional post, no grounding in what Irina built -- NEW

**Updated stats (2026-02-16)**:
- Builder mode posts: 100% in My Top + My Best categories
- Philosopher mode posts: 100% in My Worst category (now 13 examples)
- Builder vs Philosopher remains THE strongest predictor of performance

**Rule**: Your audience follows the BUILDER, not the philosopher.

**Before posting, ask**: "What did I BUILD/FIND/DO?" If you can't answer with something specific, reframe or kill the idea.

### Never Do This:

1. **English posts**
   - Evidence: Vibe Coding post = WORST performer ever (catastrophic)
   - Why it fails: Audience is Spanish-speaking, violates audience contract
   - Status: NEVER post in English on LinkedIn

2. **"Reflexion de media noche" framing**
   - Evidence: Consistent low performance
   - Why it fails: Signals random thought, not structured insight
   - Status: KILL this framing immediately

3. **Encyclopedia lists without grounding**
   - Evidence: "7 AI Concepts" (My Worst), "Muchas apps con IA solo usan la mitad del poder de los prompts" (My Worst), "La autonomia: the ultimate goal de la IA" (My Worst) -- NEW
   - Why it fails: No "I used this when..." connection, presents as expert not learner
   - Status: Every concept needs personal anchor. Now confirmed with 3 examples. PATTERN LOCKED.

4. **Borrowed authority / Borrowed observations**
   - Evidence: "Idealizamos otras latitudes" (My Worst) -- starts from reading someone else's post
   - Evidence: "Un nino que nazca hoy" (My Worst) -- abstract observation, not personal
   - Evidence: "Siempre que escucho a Geoffrey Hinton, es inevitable no quedarme reflexiva" (My Worst) -- reflecting on famous person's ideas -- NEW
   - Why it fails: Audience wants YOUR experience, not borrowed wisdom or third-party commentary
   - Status: Reference others only if tied to your specific experience. Now confirmed with 3 examples. PATTERN LOCKED.

5. **Generic motivation**
   - Evidence: "Alejarte del ruido" with sunset photo (My Worst)
   - Why it fails: Could be posted by anyone, no tech value, zero Irina voice
   - Status: NO generic life advice without tech substance

6. **Philosopher mode**
   - Evidence: Abstract concepts without doing/building anchor fail consistently (13 examples in My Worst)
   - Why it fails: Audience wants to see you BUILD, not think about building
   - Status: Always ground in specific action taken

7. **Company commentary without personal action** (NEW)
   - Evidence: "Anthropic se puso la 10 con respecto a los Ads" (My Worst)
   - Why it fails: Commenting on what a company did is not the same as building. No personal stake, no specific action taken.
   - Status: If commenting on industry news, ALWAYS tie to something you built/tried/experienced

8. **Event recaps without technical takeaway**
   - Evidence: "Solo en San Francisco / OpenClaw" (My Worst)
   - Why it fails: "I was at an event" without teaching something specific is a flex, not value
   - Status: Event posts need a concrete insight or lesson, not just "I was there"

9. **Abstract "simplicity" or meta-philosophy posts** (NEW)
   - Evidence: "Stop chasing complexity. Tu cerebro ama complicar lo simple." (My Worst)
   - Why it fails: Opens with English phrase, then philosophizes about simplicity using historical analogies (gravity, germs) instead of personal tech experience. Could be posted by anyone.
   - Status: Philosophy about how to think is NOT the same as showing what you built

10. **Promotional/CTA-first posts without value** (NEW)
    - Evidence: "Tu idea podria construirse en vivo este viernes con AI tools" (My Worst)
    - Why it fails: Asks audience to participate in something but offers no value upfront. Pure promotion without "here's what I built/learned" hook.
    - Status: Always lead with value. Promotion can be the CTA, never the hook.
