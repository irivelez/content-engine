## DESIGN RECOMMENDATIONS

### Colors
- **Background:** White or very light gray
- **CTA Button:** High-contrast (orange, teal, or electric blue)
- **Text:** Dark gray (not pure black)
- **Accents:** One color for arrows (→) and highlights

### Spacing
- Generous white space between sections
- Each pain point as its own block with breathing room
- Curriculum items clearly separated

### Mobile Optimization
- Test on phone BEFORE publishing
- CTA button should be thumb-sized
- Headlines should fit on one screen
