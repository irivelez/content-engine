## 1. Purpose

Position Irina as **the strategic translator for LATAM executives** navigating AI adoption. The diagnostic does three things simultaneously:

1. **Delivers immediate value** — a personalized archetype + 90-day roadmap in 3 minutes (the "gift" test: user gets value before doing work)
2. **Captures segmented leads** — email + archetype tag drives differentiated welcome sequences
3. **Pre-qualifies high-ticket leads** — Implementador archetype routes directly to consultation booking

This replaces PDF downloads as the primary lead magnet. Expected performance: 12x conversion vs PDF baseline (quiz funnels convert at ~47% vs PDF at ~3.8%).

**Strategic positioning**: Only Irina can build this for Spanish-speaking LATAM executives. Allie K Miller can't replicate it in Spanish. This is a Dharmesh-style moat — technical leverage applied to audience leverage.

- Manager / Líder de equipo (maps to Base-high)

**"¿Cuál es el tamaño de tu organización?"**
- 1-10 personas
- 11-50 personas

- Tecnología / Software / SaaS

**"¿Cómo describirías el uso actual de AI en tu organización?"**
- "Algunas personas usan ChatGPT por su cuenta, sin dirección oficial" → Nivel 0

**"¿Cuál es tu objetivo #1 con AI en los próximos 12 meses?"**
- Reducir costos operativos
- Aumentar velocidad de ejecución
- Ventaja competitiva (ganar mercado)

- Menos de $10K USD
- $10K - $50K USD

### Q7 — Timeline pressure
**"¿Cuándo necesitas mostrar resultados?"**
- Este trimestre
- En 6 meses

### Q8 — Biggest obstacle
**"¿Cuál es tu principal obstáculo hoy?"**
- Skills técnicos del equipo

### Q9 — Intent

## 5. The 4 Archetypes

Scoring logic uses **weighted combination** of Q1 (role), Q2 (company size), Q4 (maturity self-assessment), Q6 (budget), and Q8 (obstacle) to assign archetype.

### Explorador AI (Nivel 0-1)
**Trigger**: Q4 = Nivel 0 or 1, OR Q6 = <$10K, OR Q8 = "skills técnicos"
**Profile**: Curious but unsure. Experimenting without structure. Needs foundation before strategy.
**Recommended next step**: Sprint AI course (5-day email course, free)
**Secondary**: 1-page Maturity Framework download
**Volume expectation**: ~40% of quiz completers

### Adoptador Táctico (Nivel 1-2)
**Trigger**: Q4 = Nivel 1 or 2, AND Q6 = $10-50K, AND Q8 ≠ "skills técnicos"
**Profile**: Already using tools. Buying licenses but not measuring. Stuck in tactical execution without strategic framework.
**Recommended next step**: 1-page Framework + targeted consultation
**Secondary**: Sprint AI (if they want to level up the team)
**Volume expectation**: ~30% of quiz completers

### Implementador Estratégico (Nivel 2-3)
**Trigger**: Q4 = Nivel 2 or 3, AND Q6 = $50K+, AND Q1 = VP/Director/CXO or Founder/CEO
**Profile**: Has vision. Has budget. Needs execution support. **This is the high-ticket lead.**
**Recommended next step**: **Consultation call** (pre-qualified, route to calendar booking)
**Secondary**: ROI Calculator download, 1-page Framework
**Volume expectation**: ~20% of quiz completers
**Revenue note**: This archetype is where $2K-10K engagements come from. Treat every Implementador result as a sales lead.

### Arquitecto AI-Nativo (Nivel 3-4)
**Trigger**: Q4 = Nivel 3 or 4, AND Q1 = VP/Director/CXO or Founder/CEO
**Profile**: Ahead of the curve. Already building. Needs peers, not teaching. Potential case study or community co-founder.
**Recommended next step**: Peer community / mastermind (future paid offering — capture interest now)
**Secondary**: "Share your case — interview opportunity"
**Volume expectation**: ~10% of quiz completers

## 6. Personalized Results Page Structure

Every archetype result follows the same 6-part structure for consistency:

### Part 1 — Identity-forward headline
**"Eres un [Archetype]"** in large type, with a 1-line descriptor.

Example (Implementador):
> # Eres un Implementador Estratégico
> *Tienes la visión y el presupuesto. Lo que necesitas es ejecución.*

### Part 2 — Your current state (2 sentences)
Based on Q4 maturity level + Q1 role + Q2 company size.

Example (Implementador at 500-person telecom):
> Tu organización ya está en Nivel 2-3: hay procesos automatizados, AI informa al menos una decisión de negocio importante. Estás en el 15% superior de empresas LATAM, pero la distancia entre tu nivel actual y Nivel 4 es donde se define el próximo ciclo competitivo.

### Part 3 — What holds you back (1 sentence)
Based on Q8 obstacle.

Example (obstacle = "gestión del cambio"):
> Tu bloqueo no es técnico ni presupuestal — es organizacional. Los equipos adoptan herramientas; las organizaciones adoptan capacidades.

### Part 4 — Your 90-day roadmap (3 steps)
Specific, time-bound, achievable. Tailored per archetype.

Example (Implementador):
> **Día 1-30**: Audita tus 3 workflows de mayor impacto. Documenta métricas pre-AI.
> **Día 31-60**: Mueve 1 piloto a producción con gobernanza (políticas de datos + responsable + auditoría).
> **Día 61-90**: Presenta a tu comité de liderazgo: nivel actual, nivel objetivo 12 meses, inversión requerida.

### Part 5 — Recommended resources (tailored list)
- **Explorador**: Sprint AI (hero), 1-page Framework, newsletter
- **Adoptador**: 1-page Framework (hero), ROI Calculator, Sprint AI
- **Implementador**: **Book a call** (hero CTA), 1-page Framework for boardroom, ROI Calculator
- **Arquitecto**: Peer community waitlist (hero), case study interview opportunity

### Part 6 — Primary CTA (single, archetype-specific)
One clear next step per archetype. No menu of options. No "explore more." Single action.

## 7. Technical Implementation Options

### Option A — Typeform Logic + Zapier → Beehiiv
**Cost**: ~$20/mo (Typeform Business) + ~$20/mo (Zapier) = $40/mo
**Build time**: 4-6 hours
**Pros**:
- Fastest to launch
- Native logic jumps
- Mobile-optimized out of the box
- Integrates with Beehiiv for email segmentation
- Analytics built in
**Cons**:
- Limited customization of results page (can use redirect to custom URL as workaround)
- Feels like "a Typeform" — less distinctive
- Brand ceiling — you're renting infrastructure
**Verdict**: **RECOMMENDED for launch**. Ship now, iterate on data.

### Option B — Interact Quizzes
**Cost**: ~$27/mo
**Build time**: 4-6 hours
**Pros**:
- Built specifically for quiz funnels
- Native archetype/result mechanics
- Better results page customization than Typeform
- Built-in email capture optimization
**Cons**:
- Another tool to learn
- Brand still rented
- Less flexible than custom build long-term
**Verdict**: Not worth migrating from Option A to B — if we're going to invest in migration, skip to Option C.

### Option C — Custom HTML + Claude-powered agent (Dharmesh pattern)
**Cost**: Hosting (~$5-20/mo) + Claude API (~$20-50/mo at moderate volume)
**Build time**: 8-10 hours initial build, plus iteration
**Pros**:
- **The unfair advantage**. No one else in LATAM-Spanish AI content has this.
- Fully customized results (Claude generates personalized roadmap per user based on all 10 answers — not just archetype template)
- Owns the brand experience end-to-end
- Can evolve into full strategic advisor ("Irina's AI twin")
- Defensible moat — hard to replicate without technical depth
**Cons**:
- Longest to build
- Requires maintenance
- Variable cost scales with traffic (manageable with caching)
**Verdict**: **Migration target after Option A validates demand.** Build this once you have >500 quiz completions and confirmed demand signal.

### Recommended Path

```
Week 1 (2026-04-21):  Launch Option A (Typeform + Zapier + Beehiiv)
Weeks 2-4:             Collect 200+ completions. Measure archetype distribution.
                       Validate hypothesis that Implementador leads convert to calls.
Month 2:               If validated → build Option C (custom Claude agent)
                       If not validated → optimize Option A or pivot positioning
```

**Decision gate for Option C migration**:
- 500+ quiz completions
- 15%+ of Implementador archetype book a call
- At least 1 paid engagement sourced from the quiz

## 12. What NOT to Do

- **No "lose weight" guru copy.** This audience left guru energy behind. Stay builder-forward, specific, confident.
- **No "sign up" or "register" language.** Frame as "send me my diagnostic."
- **No generic results.** If a user gets a result that sounds like it could apply to anyone, we've failed. Every result should feel specific.
- **No blank templates in the resources.** Completed examples only.
- **No multiple CTAs per result page.** One primary action per archetype.
- **No "are you sure?" confirmation for exits.** Respect the user's time. If they leave, they leave.
- **No video gating.** The diagnostic is the product. Don't add friction.
- **No forcing Sprint AI on Implementadores.** They want consultation, not a free course.

## 14. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Low completion rate (<50%) | Medium | Pre-test with 5 users. Cut 1-2 questions if needed. |
| Archetype results feel generic | High | Invest 2-3 hours in specific copy per archetype. Use real LATAM examples. |
| Implementadores don't book calls | Medium | Offer 15-min "orientation call" instead of 60-min "consultation" — lower friction. |
| Quiz feels like marketing, not diagnostic | High | Validate tone with 3 ICP-1 users before launch. Kill any question that feels like lead scoring. |
| Email deliverability issues | Low | Warm up Beehiiv sender. Use custom domain SPF/DKIM. |
| Scaling cost if viral | Low | Typeform scales automatically. Claude API would need rate limiting in Option C. |

## 15. Irina's Voice Guardrails (Applied Throughout)

- **Tú form always.** Never usted.
- **Builder-forward.** "Construí", "implementé", "medí" — not "discutimos" or "analizamos."
- **Specific numbers.** "El 65% de empresas LATAM" beats "la mayoría de empresas."
- **Frontier signal.** Mention SF when relevant, not performatively.
- **Zero buzzwords.** No "sinergia," "ecosistema," "paradigma," "holístico."
- **Directness over diplomacy.** "Tu bloqueo no es técnico" beats "podrías considerar que el reto no es necesariamente técnico."
- **Authority without arrogance.** Show the work. Never lecture.
- **The "forwardable" test.** Would Camila forward this result to her CEO? If no, rewrite.
