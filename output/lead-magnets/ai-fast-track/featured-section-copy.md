## 1. Positioning principle (Lara Acosta rule)

> **"Free LinkedIn Profile Audit" outperforms "Free LinkedIn Profile Tips" every time.**

Translation: the Featured section must promise a **benefit**, not describe a **topic**. Every title and description below passes this test — each one names the *outcome* the reader gets, not the subject matter of the course.

Additional rules applied:
- **Short titles** — scan in 1 second on mobile (≤7 words)
- **Active verbs** — "Domina", "Empieza", "Conviértete" (not "Aprende", "Descubre")
- **Front-loaded hook** — the first 3 words carry 80% of the click
- **No fluff** — no "¡Hola!", no "Si quieres...", no conditional framing
